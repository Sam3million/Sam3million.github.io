This was a laser-mirror puzzle game I made for the 2021 Technology Student Association video game design competition.
Our team consisted of 6 members. I handled the Unity work and programming, and assisted with level design and story.
Other members worked on level design, 3D modeling, UI, sounds, and story.
This was the first project I worked on where everything seemed to click.
It was here I learned how to effectively work in a team development environment. 
We ended up winning 1st place in the Florida state conference and 2nd place nationally!
Download it <a href="https://irivium.itch.io/arid-sun">here</a>!
